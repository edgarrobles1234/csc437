Morel Mushroom

Wikipedia

Type: Mushroom

Season: Spring

Found in forested areas. Safe to eat when correctly identified and cooked.

Chanterelle

Wikipedia

Type: Mushroom

Season: Summer to Fall

Usually found in forests. Harvest lightly and identify carefully.

Wild Blackberry

Wikipedia

Type: Berry

Season: Summer

Found along trails and field edges. Leave some berries for wildlife.

Miner's Lettuce

Wikipedia

Type: Leafy Green

Season: Spring

Found in shady, moist areas. Pick gently and avoid uprooting the plant.

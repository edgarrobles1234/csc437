Morel Mushroom

Wikipedia: Morchella

Type: Mushroom

Scientific Name: Morchella

Best Season: Spring

Safe to Eat: Yes, if correctly identified and cooked

Found In: Forested areas, especially near hardwood trees.

Harvest Notes: Cut carefully at the base and avoid damaging nearby growth.

Sustainability Notes: Only take what you need and leave smaller mushrooms behind.

Chanterelle

Wikipedia: Chanterelle

Type: Mushroom

Scientific Name: Cantharellus

Best Season: Summer to Fall

Safe to Eat: Yes, if correctly identified

Found In: Forested areas, especially mossy conifer and mixed forests.

Harvest Notes: Cut or gently twist at the base and brush off dirt in the field.

Sustainability Notes: Harvest lightly and avoid disturbing the surrounding forest floor.

Wild Blackberry

Wikipedia: Rubus fruticosus

Type: Plant / Berry

Scientific Name: Rubus fruticosus

Best Season: Summer

Safe to Eat: Yes, if collected away from polluted areas

Found In: Trails, roadsides, field edges, and open wooded areas.

Harvest Notes: Pick ripe berries by hand and avoid damaging the plant.

Sustainability Notes: Leave some berries for wildlife and future regrowth.

Miner's Lettuce

Wikipedia: Claytonia perfoliata

Type: Plant / Leafy Green

Scientific Name: Claytonia perfoliata

Best Season: Spring

Safe to Eat: Yes, when properly identified

Found In: Shady, moist areas in California.

Harvest Notes: Pick leaves gently and avoid uprooting the whole plant.

Sustainability Notes: Harvest lightly so plants can continue growing.
